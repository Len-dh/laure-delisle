Laure is a Research Engineer in the London based AI-for-Good team at Element AI. Prior to joining Element AI, Laure worked on applying Data Science for CyberSecurity at Airbus Defence, the French Atomic Energy Commission and more recently Lastline. She holds a Master of Data Science from Illinois Tech (Chicago) and a Master in Computer Science and Computer Engineering with a minor in IT management from ESIEA (Ecole Superieure d'Informatique, Electronique, Automatique - Paris).
Her research interests span non-supervised learning for CyberSecurity, Transfer Learning and Domain Adaptation for Machine Learning. When not helping NGOs use ML for social good, she dedicates her extra-time to increasing diversity in tech and building ImpactNet.
